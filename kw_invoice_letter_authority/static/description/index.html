Module settings

The first step is to install the modules Letter Authority Invoice for Ukraine, Partner requisites for accounting, Partner requisites visualization. To do this, go to the Main menu/Applications and search for Letter Authority Invoice and Partner requisites in turn, click Install.

The Partner requisites for accounting, Partner requisites visualization modules add information about the Identity document to the Private information tab.

To fill in the passport data and display it in the document, fill in the data on the Private information tab of the contacts. To do this, go to the Main menu / Employees, open the card of the required employee, click Edit and fill in the information on the Private information tab. When you are done, click Save.

To obtain a power of attorney, create a new Supplier Account, go to Main menu/Accounts/Suppliers/Accounts and click Create.

Fill in the Supplier account fields as usual. Go to the Power of Attorney tab and fill in the fields:
1) date and invoice number;
2) number of the power of attorney;
3) employee to whom it was issued;
4) date of issue of the power of attorney;
5) the date the power of attorney is valid;
6) elect a director;
7) select the chief accountant.
